What is Punishing Gray Raven?


Punishing Gray Raven is an Role-playing, Adventure, and Visual Novel Game.

It is known as a gacha game, wherein you would have to spend in-game currency to achieve wanted characters. It is developed by Kuro Game and was released on May 28, 2020. The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to a robotic virus called the Punishing. The characters can be pulled through this system that is called the gacha system. The game has a unique combat system that combines fast-paced action with strategic elements. Players can switch between different characters on the fly, each with their own abilities and playstyles.
